What's inside

A full neuro-symbolic tutoring loop — pedagogy you can trust, because the math is verified.

🧠

Socratic hints

Five escalating levels of guidance — from "what kind of problem is this?" to a fully worked walkthrough — that never reveal the final answer too early.

✅

SymPy verification

A symbolic computer-algebra engine solves and checks every step deterministically. The AI guides; SymPy is the source of truth.

✍️

Handwriting recognition

Write a problem on the whiteboard and submit — a vision model transcribes your handwriting into an expression the tutor can work with.

💬

Claude-powered chat

Discuss any problem in natural language with a Claude model of your choice. Grounded in the verified solution, so it stays accurate.

🎨

Flexible whiteboard

Draw on the built-in canvas or switch to the Excalidraw engine — and expand either to fullscreen for more room to think.

📈

Animated solutions

See the math come alive: SymPy-powered plots and step animations turn an abstract equation into something you can watch unfold.

How it works

From a scribble on the board to a verified, guided solution.

Write it down

Sketch your equation on the whiteboard, or just type it in.

Recognise & parse

Handwriting is transcribed and the expression is classified.

Verify with SymPy

The engine solves it exactly and produces checked steps.

Guide, don't tell

The tutor turns those steps into Socratic hints and a chat you can question.

Under the hood

A standalone frontend talking to a FastAPI backend — no build step required.
